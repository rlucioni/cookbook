# Manhattan

Makes 1 cocktail.

### Ingredients

- 2 ounces rye whiskey
- 3/4 ounces sweet vermouth
- dash of Angostura bitters
- 1/4 teaspoon maraschino cherry syrup
- 1 maraschino cherry

### Instructions

1. Combine with ice and stir well.
2. Strain over maraschino cherry into a chilled cocktail glass.
